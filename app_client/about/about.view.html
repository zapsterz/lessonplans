The idea

So here's the thing. We have a national curriculum. That's right, it's NATIONAL.

So therefore - we should have national lesson plans right? Yes? No. Not at the moment anyway, and that's the dream.

We aim to provide fully approved, fully editable plans + schemes of work, for every year group, and everything on the national curriculum.

Sound a big idea? We hope so. If we get it right, we'll change teaching as we know it.

Progress so far

Hmm, yeah. So right now not a lot of progress really.

But there are big plans. Yep, BIG PLANS. Here are some of them:

1. Get the Government on board

This is essential, as we want Offstead on board. Yep, love them or hate them, we NEED them.

2. Get the schemes of work

These can then be reviewed by Offstead. We want every single thing on this site to have an Offstead rating.

This gives us credibility, and more importantly, trust.

When you download a plan from this site, we want you to know that it's been reviewed, approved, and is ready-to-go.
